Validate Diploma

Companies need to ensure that certificates presented by job candidates are correct and valid as they are a crucial factor in determining the candidate's qualifications and suitability for the role. Certificates provide evidence of the candidate's education, training, and professional qualifications, which are essential for any job. Hiring an employee with false or misleading certificates can result in serious consequences for the company, such as legal liabilities, damage to reputation, and loss of business. ValidateIt provides companies with a portal to validate the Education details provided by any applicant by using Blockchain technology to ensure authenticity and ZERO tampering of data provided, thereby helping them make the correct decisions.
